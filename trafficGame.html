Traffic Jam

About

Traffic Jam is a 3D endless runner where the player must dodge traffic and collect as many coins and score as they can. On death, the user gets an updated high-score if they manage to beat their previously saved score and have coins to purchase new customization.

The core gameplay loop is simple; Beat your previous high-score, collect as many coins as you can and customize your character through in-game purchases. In this project, I made use of 3D mathematics, common software engineering design patterns, arrays, loops and object instantiation alongside multiple performance enhancing techniques
