Zombie Survival

Zombie Survival is a round-based zombie survival game inspired from Call of Duty: Zombies. The core gameplay loop: The Player must survive as long as they can, surviving a hoard of zombies. Every time the player damages the zombie, they earn points which they will be able to use towards upgrading their equipment or purchasing perks.

During this project, I spent a large amount of time not only coding, but also working and expanding my knowledge with 3D modelling. I used multiple common design patterns throughout the development process and made use of Vector mathematics, Quaternions and other 3D mathematics. Alongside this, I developed Zombie AI using Unity navmesh and a state machine.
